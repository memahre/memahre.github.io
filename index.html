Research

Multimodal Model Evaluation and Interpretability

Problem

Language to vision and language to image models have been researched, but an underexplored area is the direct comparison of audition and vision models to analyze representational alignment.

Data

AudioCaps2.0 dataset

Method

Utilize the matching audio and text captions to generate matching images with stable diffusion. Extract the embeddings for image and audio to run through respective foundation model (BEATs, DINOvs) to then use similarity analysis (CKA, RSA) to compare representational similarity across modalities.

Communication & Health Behavior (Survey Evaluation)

Problem

Health behaviors are shaped not only by individual psychology, but also by how information is communicated. This project evaluated how communication and behavioral theory–informed messaging related to changes in health attitudes/behaviors.

Data

Pre- and post-test survey data from approximately 500 students.

Method

Study design and variable construction, use quantitative analysis of behavioral and communication measures using SPSS and R, and interpret results using behavioral change theories.

Outcome

Produced a research-driven evaluation of patterns in survey responses and connected results back to behavioral theory. Received a significant positive outcome.
